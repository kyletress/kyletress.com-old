Thoughts on the selections.

A Song of Ice and Fire has been consistently keeping me up until 2am. If you watch the television show, you will probably enjoy the books. It’s like having the next 6 seasons available RIGHT NOW! You’ll be in for some work though. The cast of characters number in the thousands, as does the page count.

I read Film School in one sitting. Going to USC Film School is a dream of mine. Steve Bowman did it at the age of 40, and he sold a television show to CBS in the process. Engaging.

App Empire and The Art of the App Store are must-reads if you want to own an app business. To be better at my own app business, I’m teaching myself to write iPhone and iPad apps. Programming in Objective-C and iOS Programming are the two must have books on the subject. Both are surprisingly readable.

It’s the 100th anniversary of the sinking of R.M.S. Titanic and it’s still a fascinating subject. Discovery Channel had it well covered in April but a Night to Remember has depth and scope unlike anything seen on TV.

I always try to read a few books in the religion/atheism/skepticism/humanism genre. Why are you Atheists So Angry is a hilarious take on the subject and beautifully written.

Dr. Dan Roling recommended the Power of Habit. Looking forward to reading this one as we get closer to the start of the season.
