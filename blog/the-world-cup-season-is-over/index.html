The World Cup season is over

I finished 16th in the final World Cup race of the season. Konigssee has never been one of my strong tracks. I had trouble out of the groove on run one, and then through bendaway and kreisel on run two. It’s a disappointing finish but I ended up a career best 12th overall in the 2013/2014 World Cup rankings. In Sochi I’ll have a chance to replace my worst finish (Calgary) with my result from the Games, so I’m hoping to jump into the top 10 for next season.

I want to congratulate my teammate Matt Antoine on finishing third overall in the World Cup rankings, as well as Martins Dukurs on winning his second Triple Trophy. It’s an honor racing with these guys.
