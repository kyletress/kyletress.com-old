Why does your website look so plain?

My site came to look this way when I realized two important things; first, I’m the only person who cares about the design, and second, I don’t care that much. There is something appealing in the simplicity of an unstyled website, so I undesigned to focus on my content and what Meditations represents as a whole. Simplicity is too often undervalued.

I have written a great deal about modern personal websites, from the technical knowledge needed to build them to their value in an increasingly noisy world. Now more than ever, they are important digital properties and I want to encourage people to learn the skills needed to build and maintain them on their own.
