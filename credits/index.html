My humble site is named after one of my favorite books, Meditations by Marcus Aurelius. It’s minimal by design, as I believe personal websites are the autobiographies of the digital age and should reflect their owner’s personality. There is a lot of content – you’ll just need to dig a little.

The site is powered by Siteleaf and hosted on GitHub. You can view the source code if you’re into that.

All photos are mine unless otherwise noted. My headshot is courtesy of Damian Battinelli. The font is Open Sans delivered by Google Fonts.

I work on a MacBook Air. For visual design I use Sketch and I connect to a Nitrous.io box. I have written more about my setup here.

The site makes use of CSS3 features like Flexbox, transitions, and transforms. Therefore it is optimized for IE10 and up.

I'm on twitter, facebook, youtube, github, treehouse, and instragram.
