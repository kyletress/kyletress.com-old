Fantasy Sliding

FantasySliding is a Ruby on Rails fantasy sports league I designed, developed, and deployed in early 2014. I was responsible for the entire site, from concept to code.

It’s a prediction game for bobsled and skeleton. The rules are simple: join a league and predict the top ten finishers of each race on your league schedule. You get 3 points for a correct guess, 2 points for a guess that’s one spot away, and 1 point for a guess that’s two spots away. You can create your own leagues, invite friends, and choose your own FIBT races to play. The game is free and will be available in limited beta this fall, and open to the public in January 2015.
