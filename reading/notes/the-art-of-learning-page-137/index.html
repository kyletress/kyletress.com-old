Summary

For much of this book I have described my vision of the road to mastery — you start with the fundamentals, get a solid foundation fueled by understanding the principles of your discipline, then you expand and refine your repetoire, guided by your individual predispositions, while keeping in touch, however abstractly, with what you feel to be the essential core of the art.
