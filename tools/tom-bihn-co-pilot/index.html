Tom Bihn Co-pilot

Tom Bihn was kind enough to send me some gear last season, and I fell in love with this bag immediately. I use it to hold and transport my everyday stuff; my MacBook Air, Kindle, favorite book, current book, notebook, and more. It’s also a great airplane carry-on if you don’t need to pack clothes. If you do, the Aeronaut (which I also own and love) is equally impressive.

I’ve had a lifelong obsession with bags which I’ve only recently started to break. This one won’t be going anywhere anytime soon. It could easily be my one bag.
