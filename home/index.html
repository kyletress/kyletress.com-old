What's happening this week

  • Michio Kaku on The Daily Show discussing his excellent book The Future of the Mind. “We are in the Golden Age of brain research”.

  • Humans need not apply. A video of the coming robot revolution. Neither good nor bad, just inevitable.

  • IBM has announced TrueNorth, a brain-like chip consisting of 4096 cores, 1 million neurons, and 5.4 billion transistors. It’s the most massively parallel chip in the world, but still pales in comparison to the human brain. However with TrueNorth running software like Deep Neural Networks, scientists are closer than ever to creating a brain on a chip.
