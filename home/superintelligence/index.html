The Superintelligence Reading Group

I’ve signed up for the Superintelligence reading group, hosted by the Machine Intelligence Research Institute. The group will meet virtually once a week to discuss readings on Nick Bostrom’s new book, Superintelligence: Paths, Dangers, Strategies. As I’ve mentioned before, artificial intelligence is one of my favorite topics and likely one of the most important problems of the 21st century.
