Once a month, I send a short email of book recommendations to friends and devoted readers. If you enjoy reading as much as I do, I would love to have you as a subscriber. Each recommendation comes with a brief review and thoughts on why I chose the book.

Subscribe

My mom was a reading teacher for 40 years. She taught me the importance of being both widely-read and well-read1. I try to reflect this breadth in my recommendations. You’ll find classics, philosophy, history, biography, business, psychology, technology, and books on performance and potential among others.

Here’s my promise:

  • I will never spam you.
  • You can unsubscribe anytime.
  • The email comes from my personal email address.
  • I will respond to every email I receive.
  • These are books to change your life, not necessarily what’s popular or bestselling.
  • I personally read each recommendation.

The first mailing will go out on April 30th, 2015.

  1. As Mortimer Adler discussed in his classic How to Read a Book, these are not synonymous. ↩
