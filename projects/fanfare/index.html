Fanfare

I started Fanfare in 2014 from my apartment in Lake Placid with the mission to help amateur athletes fund their careers. It has since grown into the #1 site for recurring and one-time financial support for athletes.

Fanfare is a simple service. Athletes can easily integrate a donate button on their website or facebook page. Our service allows fans to donate once or on a recurring basis. You can log in and see all your fans and donations, email them, send them updates, or manage their memberships. Fanfare is free for athletes. We take a small percentage of donations to cover the cost of transactions (managed through Stripe) and our server costs.
