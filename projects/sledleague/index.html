SledLeague

SledLeague is a fantasy sports prediction app for Bobsled/Skeleton that is currently in development. I am building the app in Ruby on Rails, and I am responsible for its design, development, and maintenance. The source code is on GitHub.

History

One of my first Ruby on Rails apps when I was learning the framework was a fantasy Formula One game. I knew that I wanted to create a similar game for the sports of bobsled and skeleton, but at the time the app was too ambitious. I finally returned to the idea in 2013.

The App

SledLeague has a simple premise. A user predicts the top finishers for a race, and they are awarded points for how close their predictions are to the final result. But that simplicity hides a lot of complexity and functionality, from the difficulty of managing timezones to league-specific rule sets.

Technology and Features

  • Private and public leagues
  • League specific rules and race selections
  • Integration with SendGrid to deliver weekly updates on the status of your league.
