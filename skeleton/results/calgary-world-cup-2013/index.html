Calgary World Cup

The worst start to a World Cup season I’ve had in years. As I said in media appearances and press conferences leading up to the Games, this was the moment I decided I was going to go the Olympics. I could focus on this result and dwell on it, or forget it and move on. I’m glad I chose the latter.
