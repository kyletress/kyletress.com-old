Igls World Cup

This was the final World Cup race of the 2014 season. I had enough points to qualify for the Olympic team at this point, but I was really looking for a strong result here in Igls. I wanted to prove to myself that I could compete, even with a slower push.

Run One

I made a mistake out of kreisel on this run. I was a little high on the exit and I hit the left wall. It’s not an uncommon mistake, but it cost me several positions.

Run Two

This was a much better run for me, and closer to where I should have been on run one. Had I not made a mistake out of Kreisel on run one, I think I had a shot at a podium position finish. Regardless, I was really happy with this race, and it gave me the points I needed to lock the Olympic spot.
