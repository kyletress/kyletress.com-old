Sochi Winter Olympic Games

I’ve written a much longer essay about my experience at the Winter Olympic Games, but in short, it was the accomplishment of a lifelong dream. While I outwardly accomplished my goal, I was disappointed with the result. I focused on the wrong things and convinced myself for too long that simply going was enough. It wasn’t. It has provided great motivation for my 2018 training.
