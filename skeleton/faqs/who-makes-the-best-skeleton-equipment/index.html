Who makes the best skeleton equipment?

Equipment is largely personal preference so you’ll have to experiment. The only sled and runner makers I can personally recommend are Protostar Engineering (not available for sale), Willi Scheider, Richard Bromley, Ryan Davenport, Don Hass, and Andi Walser.
