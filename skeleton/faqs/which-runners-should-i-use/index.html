Which runners should I use?

The answer comes with a lot of testing and experience. Don’t be afraid to try things that scare you a little bit. Write everything down. You’ll build confidence in your equipment and eventually learn to make the right choice 98% of the time. I recommend every skeleton athlete read through Ryan Davenport’s website for a thorough introduction to how runners and how specific cuts affect control.
