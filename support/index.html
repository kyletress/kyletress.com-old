Weekly sponsorships

I offer one “mini” sponsorship each week to individuals, products, or companies. Sponsorships are available in-season and off-season. A sponsor receives:

  • a thank you tweet at the end of the week.
  • a thank you post on my Facebook page.
  • a brief post on my website at the beginning of the week.
  • a small banner at the top of my site for the entire week.
  • a thank you message in my weekly newsletter.

In-season subscribers also receive:

  • logo placement on equipment (races are televised).

Mini-sponsorships are a great way to test the waters before jumping into a longer-term commitment. The International Olympic Committee recently eased the rules regarding athlete promotion of personal sponsors during the Olympics. As a additional benefit, I will give weekly sponsors priority going into the 2018 Games.

Sponsorships are available for $250/week. You can book by emailing kyle@kyletress.com.
