Support

My skeleton career and my writing are fan supported. I rely on the financial support of readers and fans like you to continue training for the 2018 Games and to continue publishing great material on my site. There are 2 main ways you can help:

  1. Become a member of my site
  2. Sponsor my training and writing for a week

Become a member

By becoming a member of my site, you will have exclusive access to content unavailable to regular readers, including pre-release book excerpts, bonus essays and chapters, a private newsletter, and more. Membership is handled through Memberful.com and you can cancel at anytime, no questions asked.

Support me on Patreon

By supporting me on Patreon, I’ll continue to publish great essays every week as well as new book videos on my YouTube page.

Sponsor my site

I offer week long sponsorships of my site to companies and individuals. These sponsorships carry over to my skeleton career as well, where you can receive top billing on my equipment and television coverage.
